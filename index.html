A card game for 3-5 players about flawed surveillance, impossible decisions and the people caught in between.

Bycatch is currently in development and will be released on January 1, 2015. Supply will be limited so pre-order your copy now!

In Bycatch players each control a nation's intelligence, defense and humanitarian efforts. Using satellite surveillance they search for suspected enemies of the state hiding abroad. Once a suspect is located players attempt to eliminate them using drone strikes. Meanwhile, players try to shelter their own citizens from harm.

A preview of Bycatch's card art and design.

  • A unique and novel surveillance mechanic uses your phone's camera to let you experience the shortcomings of satellite surveillance firsthand.
  • Rich and varied card artwork helps you empathize with your citizens and the horrors of drone strikes and collateral damage.
  • Simple to learn and quick to play, Bycatch invites a wide range of players to experience the dilemmas of remote warfare and encourages heated debate during and after play.
