  • Simple to learn and quick to play, Bycatch invites a wide range of players to contemplate the dilemmas of remote warfare.
  • An inventive surveillance mechanic uses your phone's camera to let you experience the shortcomings of aerial spying firsthand.
  • Appealing artwork helps you empathize with your citizens and the horrors of drone strikes and collateral damage.

A preview of Bycatch's card artwork and design

In Bycatch players each control a nation's intelligence, defense and humanitarian efforts. Using satellite surveillance they search for suspected enemies of the state hiding abroad. Once a suspect is located, players attempt to eliminate them using drone strikes. Meanwhile, players must shelter their own citizens to keep them safe from harm.
